Hello, nice to meet you!

My name is Danilo (he/him) and I am a UI Designer working in the Game Industry since 2015. I make 2D Art, Design, and a bit of web and game programming.

This is a space I created to share personal projects and practice web development. A place to publish works that I had total ownership and hopefully show the person I am, far from works under NDA and without taking credits from many of the talented people I work with.

I'm currently working at Frontier Developments as a Senior UI Designer, where I make low-fidelity mockups alongside Game Designers, create High-Fidelity Assets with my fellow UI Artists/Designers, and help Programmers with a little bit of coding to give the UI a touch of polish.

In the past, I worked on a big MMORPG with 3-4 months patch-cycle for multiple platforms, Web Development, mobile and VR Games.

I'm particularly passionate about Pixel Art, 2D games and Typography (And UI, of course!)
